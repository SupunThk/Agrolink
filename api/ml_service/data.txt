1. Cherry_(including_sour)___Powdery_mildew

Description
A fungal disease causing white powdery patches on leaves, stems, and buds, leading to distorted growth.

Recommended Treatment

Remove infected plant parts
Improve air circulation and sunlight exposure
Apply fungicides like sulfur or potassium bicarbonate
Avoid overhead watering
